> [!TIP]
> Large Vacoule only in plant cell.

**NAME**: Vacuole  

**CHARACTERISTICS**:
- Liquid-filled sac with cell sap  
- Surrounded by tonoplast membrane  
- Large in mature plant cells  

**FUNCTION**:
- Water absorption makes cell turgid  
- Osmoregulation in unicellular organisms
