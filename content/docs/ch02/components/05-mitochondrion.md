**NAME**: Mitochondrion

(plural: mitochondria)

**CHARACTERISTICS**:
- Rod-shaped or spherical  
- **Two membrane** layers (smooth outer, folded inner)  
- Contains respiratory enzymes  

**FUNCTION**:
- Generates energy (ATP) through glucose oxidation  
- Produces ATP for cellular activities
