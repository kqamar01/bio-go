**NAME**: Chloroplast  

**CHARACTERISTICS**:
- Oval-shaped  
- Two membrane layers (just like mitochondria) 
- Contains chlorophyll in grana  

**FUNCTION**:
- Absorbs sunlight for photosynthesis  
- Converts light to chemical energy
