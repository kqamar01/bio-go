**NAME**: Lysosome  

**CHARACTERISTICS**:
- Small spherical sac  
- Single membrane  
- Contains hydrolytic enzymes  

**FUNCTION**:
- Breaks down complex molecules  
- Destroys bacteria and damaged cell parts
