> [!TIP]
> Cell wall only in plant cell !

**NAME**: Cell Wall 

**CHARACTERISTICS**:
- Strong, rigid outer layer  
- Made of cellulose fibres  
- Fully permeable  

**FUNCTION**:
- Maintains plant cell shape  
- Provides mechanical support
