# Lipid Elements <sub>[4.4.1]</sub>

## NAME
**Lipids** - hydrophobic biological molecules with diverse cellular functions

## CHARACTERISTICS
- Composed of **carbon, hydrogen, and oxygen** elements only
- **Much higher ratio** of hydrogen to oxygen compared to carbohydrates
- **Hydrophobic** - repel water due to nonpolar nature
- **Soluble** in organic solvents like alcohol, ether, and chloroform

## FUNCTION
- **Energy storage** - store more energy per gram than carbohydrates
- **Membrane structure** - form cellular membranes
- **Insulation** - protect against temperature changes
- **Signaling molecules** - hormones and communication compounds
